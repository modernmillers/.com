Hey, Lauren here!

So glad you’ve made it! Welcome to my blog, { thoroughly } Modern Millers. Let me start by telling you a little about myself. I am a San Diego native (9 generations to be exact!), graced with gorgeous weather year round, and can’t imagine living anywhere else. I grew up in a large family, with four amazing older brothers, each of whom attributed to my personality and identity.

  • The Family

  • The Brothers

I have worked as a full time nanny, while going to school for the past 8 years. After taking the slow track to get my associates degree followed by a Bachelors Degree in Human Development, I decided to go back to school one more time (I guess I just like writing papers!). Third times a charm?

I have a passion for sewing, crafting and reading. I am becoming more like my mother with each passing day! Growing up, I was the kid who went to quilting camp at Quilt in a Day (San Marcos, Ca) instead of sleep-away camp with friends. I was also the kid who gifted books for birthdays, so you can imagine my popularity level…

I have started a new chapter in my life, and I couldn’t be more excited about the future. I am newly engaged to the man of my dreams, Mr. Miller, who took me to Paris and asked for my hand in marriage (read about it in this post)! We plan on tying the knot in the new year, and watching our life unfold in front of our eyes.
